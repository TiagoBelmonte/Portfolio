Interview

I have interviewed Glenn Lambert. He is my intership coordinator and the head chief of the department helpdesk. He told me about the history of the hospital, the most difficult time and his opinion about the future of the hospital.

When was the organization founded? Who founded it? Why?

The hospital was founded in 1923. We received this from a monastic community. It is a fusion between the hospital, a maternity and a clinic. Because the organisation keep growing we decided to make one building of the three departments.


How has the organization evolved throughout the years?

What has changed the most is the work of the hospital. With the evolution of computer science we could make the inscription of a new patient a lot easier than with paper. We didn’t change location but we have grown, we now have approximately 200 places where we can work.


What was the most difficult time for the organization?

The most difficult time of the organization was the covid-19 crisis because we weren’t ready. It was very difficult for us to work from home. It was also a lot of work because we needed to program everything that is needed to work from home in days. It was also difficult for the doctors, some doctors who were not specialised to work in the intensive care had to work there because we needed all the help. Now I am happy that it is over and I touch wood that such thing don’t happens again. But if it happens we will be ready.


What will be the most important challenges for the organization in the future?

The most important challenge will be that al the person who works in the hospital and the patients who come to the hospital can work with a computer. If everyone can work with that you can make a hospital where we can help people a lot faster.


What do you expect the organization will look like in 10 years?

We expect a hospital where we can help the patient very fast with the use of computers and robots. We now have one robot that can help you search your destination inside the hospital.


What qualities/values are important for the people who works or wants to work here?

If you want to work here you need to help the patients with a smile, you need to work efficiently and for me the most important is that you can work in groups.
